Key Features

Immutable Model Storage

Models are stored on-chain as immutable records, ensuring security and integrity. Users retain full ownership of their model blueprints.

Global On-Chain Executors

A decentralized network of executors runs AI models globally, enhancing scalability and reducing latency. Select infrastructure based on your needs.

Data Privacy & Security

Data access is managed via secure keys and hashes, permissioned by you through IPFS. Decentralized storage minimizes data breach risks.

Cost-Effective & Flexible AI Deployment

By leveraging decentralized resources, AIRA significantly reduces the cost of deploying and running AI models compared to centralized services. Models can be executed locally, on the cloud, or through our global network - offering unparalleled portability and opportunities for monetization.
